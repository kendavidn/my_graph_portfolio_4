About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an eight‑week, live‑online certification that equips participants with no‑code AI tools to automate everyday academic and professional workflows.

  • Automate document, spreadsheet and data tasks
  • Organise knowledge and streamline literature reviews
  • Build custom chatbots for projects or teams
  • Create professional visuals and presentations with AI
  • Showcase your learning in a hands‑on capstone project

Key Details

Format: Live & online

Learning Style: Interactive workshops & help sessions
